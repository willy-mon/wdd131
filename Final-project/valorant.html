WHAT IS VALORANT

Created in June 2, 2022 by Riot Games, Valorant is a First Person Shooter with Hero shooter style, meaning that you can shoot or use diferent abilities from each character to win. Since the creation, Valorant has grown a lot expanding his community and they E-sport scene, having 15.33 million players when I'm writing this, and with a peak of 1.3 million simultaneous spectators in a tournament final.

HOW TO WIN

In Valorant you win when your team make 13 points, you get points winning rounds each team have five players and with the name of "attackers" and "defenders", Each round lasts at least 2 minutes, if the attackers don't plant the Spike or kill all defenders, the defenders win a round you have two more ways to win rounds, the first one and more easy, kill all your enemies. Or you can go with more strategy and plant the Spike as an attacker or defusing it if you are a defender, but what is the Spike?

THE SPIKE

The Spike is a bomb that the attackers need to plant in an already defined part of the map, when its planted the Spike would explode after 45 seconds giving the victory of the round to the attackers, but be carefull when the Spike is planted the defenders can go and defuse it, giving the victory of the round to the defenders.

GAME PLAN

As an attacker once you plant the bomb your plan is to kill all the defenders before they defuse it, or you can use strategy to stop them to go to the bomb and win the round with the explotion of the spike. As a defender when the attacker plant the Spike you need to think how to defuse the Spike before it explodes.
